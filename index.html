About Me

My name's Mubashshir Uddin, I am currently a sophomore at Indian Institute of Technology, Kanpur. I joined the Institute in 2019, Embarking upon a Bachelor's of Science in Physics, This is a four-year program, I expect to graduate in 2023

I hail from the town of Varanasi in Uttar Pradesh,India. I aspire to become an Astro-physicist, because of my emmence curiosity in particle physics, cosmology and Mathematical methods.

I also enjoy learning and doing gigs related to- Rocketry (yeah! I love SpaceX, and would love to work for them, may the opportunity arise), exploration for Extra-terrestrial life, innovation on Aerial systems and Superfast flight.

To be clear, Yeah! I am one of those nerds who have an everlasting itch for knowledge, I like to do new things, and on the way to enlightenment, often discover- new and better ways to do them. I took part in International Astronomy and astrophysics compedetion(IAAC), wherein qualifying through various stages,I stood among top 7% of all participants.

Other skills that I possess are: MATLAB, Python, C, Java, OpenCV using C/python, XFLR5, ROS, Video processing using Vegas pro, Typesetting and some basic web dev.
